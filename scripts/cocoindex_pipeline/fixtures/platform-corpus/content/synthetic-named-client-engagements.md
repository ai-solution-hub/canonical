# Named Client Engagements — Case Studies (Synthetic)

## Overview

These case studies are synthetic reference content for pipeline seam testing. They
describe engagements between the fictional supplier Ridgeway Commercial Services Ltd and
four fictional UK public-sector buyers. No real company, client, or contract information
is represented; every organisation named below is invented.

## Wyndale Metropolitan Borough Council — managed office supplies and furniture refresh

In 2024, Ridgeway Commercial Services Ltd was awarded an open tender by Wyndale
Metropolitan Borough Council for a two-year managed office-supplies and furniture-refresh
contract across the council's twelve administrative sites. The engagement consolidated
four legacy suppliers into a single ordering relationship through the StockSense Ordering
Portal, giving budget holders visibility of spend against agreed thresholds. Over the
first twelve months, next-day delivery performance held above 98 per cent, and the
GreenCycle Furniture Programme diverted more than 600 items from landfill through
refurbishment and reuse. The council's category team cited Ridgeway's ISO 9001:2015
certification and transparent monthly reporting as key factors in the award decision.

## St Aldhelm's NHS Foundation Trust — facilities and consumables framework

St Aldhelm's NHS Foundation Trust engaged Ridgeway Commercial Services Ltd under a
regional facilities and consumables framework covering three hospital sites and a network
of community clinics. The contract required compliance with infection-control procurement
standards alongside the standard quality and delivery obligations. Ridgeway's Facilities
Assurance Standard (FAS 2100) accreditation, combined with the RapidRestock Managed Print
Service for clinical documentation, supported a smooth mobilisation within the Trust's
thirty-day transition window. The Trust's estates team has since extended the relationship
into a second framework lot covering theatre consumables.

## Corvedale Academies Trust — multi-site education supplies

Corvedale Academies Trust, a multi-academy trust operating fourteen schools, selected
Ridgeway Commercial Services Ltd to consolidate stationery, classroom resources, and
furniture procurement across its estate. The trust's finance team highlighted Ridgeway's
Sustainable Supply Chain Mark (SSCM) accreditation as central to meeting its own published
sustainability commitments. Local delivery consolidation reduced vehicle movements to each
school by an estimated forty per cent in the first two terms of the contract, and the
trust's business managers now use the StockSense Ordering Portal directly for
classroom-level ordering within delegated budgets.

## Northgate Borough Council — managed office supplies and furniture refresh

In 2025, Ridgeway Commercial Services Ltd won an open tender run by Northgate Borough
Council for a two-year managed office-supplies and furniture-refresh contract spanning the
council's twelve administrative sites. The award followed a competitive evaluation in
which the council's category team weighed technical quality, delivery reliability, and
social value alongside price. Ridgeway's ISO 9001:2015 certification and its transparent
monthly reporting through the StockSense Ordering Portal were cited as decisive factors,
alongside the GreenCycle Furniture Programme's take-back commitment for the
furniture-refresh element of the contract. Next-day delivery performance for core
stationery and consumables held above 98 per cent across the engagement's first year, and
Ridgeway's designated account manager was in place within five working days of contract
award.
