# Product Catalogue — Ridgeway Commercial Services Ltd (Synthetic)

## Overview

This product catalogue is synthetic reference content for pipeline seam testing. It
describes three fictional commercial products offered by Ridgeway Commercial Services Ltd
and contains no real company or product information.

## StockSense Ordering Portal

StockSense Ordering Portal is Ridgeway's flagship ordering product: a web-based punch-out
catalogue that gives each buyer organisation controlled, budget-aware access to its
contracted product range. Key features include configurable spend-approval workflows,
role-based budget controls per cost centre, real-time order tracking, and consolidated
monthly spend reporting. StockSense Ordering Portal integrates with a buyer's finance
system to support automated purchase-order matching and electronic invoicing. The product
is deployed on every live Ridgeway contract, including the Wyndale Metropolitan Borough
Council and Corvedale Academies Trust engagements, and is licensed to buyers at no
additional cost as part of the core managed-supplies service.

## GreenCycle Furniture Programme

GreenCycle Furniture Programme is a remanufacturing and take-back product covering office
and classroom furniture. Under the programme, end-of-life furniture is collected from the
buyer's site, assessed for refurbishment, and either remanufactured for resale into a
secondary market or responsibly recycled. Buyers receive a certificate of diversion for
each collection, supporting their own sustainability reporting. GreenCycle Furniture
Programme is a named component of Ridgeway's Sustainable Supply Chain Mark (SSCM)
accreditation and was central to the furniture-refresh element of the Wyndale Metropolitan
Borough Council contract.

## RapidRestock Managed Print Service

RapidRestock Managed Print Service is a managed print and consumables product that
monitors device usage and automatically triggers replenishment orders before a site runs
out of toner or paper stock. The service includes a quarterly print-volume review,
device-level cost reporting, and a consolidated single invoice across all sites on a
contract. RapidRestock Managed Print Service is deployed at St Aldhelm's NHS Foundation
Trust, where it supports clinical documentation printing across three hospital sites and a
network of community clinics.
