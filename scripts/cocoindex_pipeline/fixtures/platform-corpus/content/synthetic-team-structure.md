# Team Structure and Key People — Ridgeway Commercial Services Ltd (Synthetic)

## Overview

This team-structure record is synthetic reference content for pipeline seam testing. It
describes the fictional leadership and departmental structure of Ridgeway Commercial
Services Ltd and contains no real personal or company information.

## Leadership team

Ridgeway Commercial Services Ltd is led by Naomi Whitfield, Managing Director, who has
overall responsibility for strategy, bid governance, and buyer relationships. Naomi is
supported by four functional leads:

- Callum Rees, Head of Compliance and Quality, owns the company's ISO 9001:2015
  certification, the Facilities Assurance Standard (FAS 2100) accreditation, and the
  Sustainable Supply Chain Mark (SSCM), and chairs the quarterly compliance review.
- Priya Anand, Head of Account Management, leads the team of named account managers
  assigned to each live contract, including the current engagements with Wyndale
  Metropolitan Borough Council, St Aldhelm's NHS Foundation Trust, and Corvedale Academies
  Trust.
- Daniel Osei, Operations Director, is responsible for the four regional fulfilment hubs,
  warehouse operations, and delivery performance against agreed key performance
  indicators.
- Grace Fenton, Head of Sustainability and Social Value, oversees the GreenCycle Furniture
  Programme, carbon reporting, and the company's social-value commitments on live
  contracts.

## Departmental structure

Beneath the leadership team, Ridgeway organises its 180 staff into four departments:
Account Management (client-facing contract delivery), Operations (warehousing, logistics,
and the regional hubs), Compliance and Quality (accreditation, audit, and continuous
improvement), and Commercial (bid writing, pricing, and framework management). Each
department reports monthly into the leadership team, and department heads jointly own the
company's bid-governance process for new tender opportunities.

## Bid and account governance

New tender opportunities are triaged jointly by the Commercial department and the relevant
functional lead before a bid decision is made. Once a contract is won, a named account
manager from Priya Anand's team is assigned within five working days, working alongside
Daniel Osei's operations team to plan mobilisation.
