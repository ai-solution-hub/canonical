## 6. Company Values and Ethos

### IMPACT Values

example-client's values are articulated through the IMPACT acronym:

| Letter | Value | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| **I** | Innovate | Bring creativity to every aspect of our work |
| **M** | Mindful | Acknowledge every voice with respect to encourage a mindful workplace |
| **P** | Proactivity | Take charge confidently; own your actions and drive change |
| **A** | Accountable | Commit wholeheartedly to excellence in all that we do |
| **C** | Collaborate | Embrace collective talents to achieve more together |
| **T** | Transparency | Encourage openness, sharing insights to build trust and clarity |

### Vision

example-client's vision is centred around clients and how the company can continue to support them to make a real difference. Key themes from their vision statement:

- "Wow" clients -- be dependable, be human (not faceless), be passionate
- Listen to clients to find out how to help
- People are the company's greatest assets
- Aim to be an extension of the client's team, not just a third party
- Anticipate client needs and continue to collaborate
- Strengthen standing as sector specialists
- **"Continue to save lives through our products"** -- a strong purpose-driven statement

### Driving Force

"Commitment to safeguarding children has always been [their] driving force."

### Culture Descriptors

- "Thinkers, makers and explorers"
- Approach work and play with "interest and experimentation"
- Use "expertise, solidarity and outlook to create meaningful digital products"
- Culture of "development, growth and productivity"
- "Place people at the heart of all they do"
- Staff report the two best things about working at example-client are: the people they work with, and the chance to make a real difference to their communities
