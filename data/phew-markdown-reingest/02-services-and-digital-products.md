## 2. Services and Digital Products

example-client creates sector-focused digital solutions that help education providers,
local authorities, and healthcare organisations streamline processes, report on
impact, and drive better results. The portfolio consists of four core product
lines:

### 2.1 Learning Management System (LMS)

**URL:** https://www.client.example/digital-products/learning-management-system/

An industry-leading safeguarding LMS proven to save professionals time and
resources. Purpose-built for the safeguarding and public sector.

**Key features:**

- Set up, run, and evaluate multi-agency safeguarding training events and
  courses
- Single, secure hub for all training needs
- Automates routine tasks (booking, reporting, certificates)
- Hosts live workshops, SCORM-compliant eLearning packages, video tutorials, and
  downloadable guides
- Content tagging by topic, audience, or urgency
- Desktop and mobile access with single sign-on
- Compliance reporting: completion rates, quiz scores, training histories
- Ready-made templates matching audit criteria (e.g. KCSIE, Prevent Duty)
- GDPR-compliant with customisable data protection features
- Self-service Knowledge Base via academy.client.example

**Sub-products / sector variants:**

- LMS for Safeguarding Partnerships
- LMS for Social Care
- LMS for Councils

**Reported results:**

- Derby & Derbyshire SCP: 630% increase in delegate bookings, 360% increase in
  course offerings
- Hampshire SCP: 20% increase in knowledge from training, 15,300+ course
  attendees
- Birmingham SCP: 95%+ reduction in time to gather data and produce management
  information reports (task reduced to under 2 minutes)
- 22% average increase in knowledge and confidence among staff

**Available via:** G-Cloud 14 framework on the UK Digital Marketplace

### 2.2 Online Audit and Reporting System

**URL:** https://www.client.example/digital-solutions/audit-system/

A flexible, time-saving, purpose-built audit platform for the safeguarding
sector.

**Key features:**

- Supports local and regional safeguarding audits including Sections 11 and
  175/157
- Live Safeguarding Audit solution with real-time tracking
- Continuous updates and improved oversight
- Automated live reports by organisation, status, and custom date ranges
- Multiple question formats with easy amendments
- Administrators can review responses anytime and track revisions for full
  transparency

**Sub-products / sector variants:**

- Online Auditing System for Safeguarding Partnerships
- Online Auditing System for Multi-Academy Trusts
- Online Auditing System for Children's Services
- Online Auditing System for Sports Associations

**Reported results:**

- 100+ clients using the example-client Audit System
- Users can save up to one month per year in administrative time
- Boost audit completion rates by up to 120%

**Case studies:**

- **Dudley Safeguarding People Partnership:** Time-saving features including
  extensive question flexibility and the ability to return to and update audits
  until submission
- **Oasis Community Learning:** A Multi-Academy Trust with 54 academies
  educating 32,000+ young people; replaced a paper-based audit method with the
  example-client system to eliminate coordination bottlenecks and enable meaningful data
  reporting

**Available via:** G-Cloud 14 framework on the UK Digital Marketplace

### 2.4 Patient Discharge Management System (PDMS)

**URL:**
https://www.client.example/digital-solutions/patient-discharge-management-system/

A healthcare software product presenting professionals with real-time patient
discharge status data.

**Key features:**

- Central store of real-time patient data for discharge status management
- Active patient dashboard viewable on all devices (big screens, desktops,
  tablets, mobiles)
- Integrates with existing patient care systems including SHREWD
- Conforms to HL7 industry standard integration model (no duplication of work)
- Hospital journey tracker (co-designed with Alzheimer's Society Innovation
  Team)
- Email status updates to named patient representatives

**Funding and development:**

- Awarded a substantial grant from the Sustainable Innovation Fund
  (November 2020) to support PDMS development
- Showcased at the "Reducing Long Hospital Stays" conference in London
  (March 2019)
- Hospital journey tracker co-developed with Alzheimer's Society for people
  living with dementia

**Reported results (Luton & Dunstable University Hospital NHS FT):**

- Reduced length of stay from 3.5 days to 2.7 days
- Equates to 14,000 additional admissions in just nine months
- Also deployed at Bedford Hospital

**Benefits stated:**

- Reduced patient length of stay
- More hospital beds available
- Increased treatment capacity
- Reduced hospital costs
- Enhanced patient experience
