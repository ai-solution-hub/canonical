## 3. Industry Positioning and Target Markets

### Primary Sectors

1. **Safeguarding** -- the core sector; safeguarding children partnerships, safeguarding adults boards, multi-agency safeguarding hubs
2. **Public sector** -- local authorities, councils, children's services
3. **Education** -- multi-academy trusts, schools, education providers
4. **Healthcare / NHS** -- hospital trusts, social care departments
5. **Sports associations** -- safeguarding in sport

### Positioning Statement

example-client positions itself as a "specialist software platform developer" providing "market leading solutions and world class customer service" for the public and health sectors. The company is described as a "pioneering software developer with over 20 years of experience in software platform design."

### Government Framework Presence

- **G-Cloud 14:** Awarded a place on the UK government's latest cloud services framework
- **Digital Marketplace:** Listed as supplier 707879 (https://www.applytosupply.digitalmarketplace.service.gov.uk/g-cloud/supplier/707879)
- Products available through G-Cloud: LMS and Audit System

### Competitive Differentiators

- Sector specialism (safeguarding-first approach rather than generic SaaS)
- 20+ years of domain expertise
- UK-only data storage
- Comprehensive compliance certifications (ISO 9001, ISO 27001, ISO 14001, Cyber Essentials Plus)
- Integrated product suite (LMS, auditing, websites) -- not just point solutions
- "Extension of your team" service philosophy rather than faceless third-party
